The three core metrics which will be compared are coverage availability (as a percentage of time), capacity throughput (Mbps) and latency (ms). 

Coverage availability determines how often the user has service, this metric is based on visibility of satellites and a high level model of the beamwidth of a given satellite. This will be compared to urban and rural 5G networks. Throughput is measured in Mbps, this is averaged over a period of time and will also be compared to urban and rural 5G throughput.
